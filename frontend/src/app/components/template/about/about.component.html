December 2019, something we have never imagined stars to spread and worldwide countries adopt social isolation measures, people need to recreate habits through the quarentine, and public administration takes a key role within the global dinamics. The political and economic crisis in addition with the lack of accurate information about virus spreading, leads the population to the insecurity and disinformation. We are 100.PY, and we have developed an avaliation method that integrates social economic data, using machine learning to integrate different variables such as Corruption Index and HDI to present an intuive score called Scorevid, a rate from 0 to 19 that describes the possibility of dealing with Corona in different countries.
